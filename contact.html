Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I cancel or change my booking?

You can cancel or change your booking by logging into your account and going to "My Trips." Cancellation policies vary by provider. Most bookings allow free cancellation within 24 hours of purchase.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption and are PCI DSS compliant. We never store your full card number, and all transactions are processed through secure payment gateways.

How do I request a refund?

To request a refund, go to "My Trips" in your account or contact our support team. Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days, depending on your payment method and the provider's policy.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes! We partner with leading travel insurance providers to offer comprehensive coverage. You can add insurance during checkout or within 14 days of your booking.
