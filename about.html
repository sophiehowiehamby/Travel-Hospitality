About TravelEase

We're on a mission to make travel accessible, affordable, and delightful for everyone.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate travelers, TravelEase was born out of frustration with complex booking processes and hidden fees. We believed there had to be a better way — a platform that puts travelers first.

Today, TravelEase serves over 2 million travelers annually, partnering with 500+ airlines, 10,000+ hotels, and car rental companies in 150+ countries. Our technology compares thousands of options in real-time to find you the best prices with complete transparency.
